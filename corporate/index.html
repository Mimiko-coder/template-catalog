Company

Built for mission-critical work.

For over two decades, Apex Engineering has delivered structural, mechanical, and electrical infrastructure for clients who cannot afford downtime. From offshore wind substations to industrial automation retrofits, our teams operate under the strictest safety and quality standards in the industry.

Headquartered in Istanbul with field offices across Ankara, Izmir, and Bucharest, we deploy 340+ engineers and technicians across EMEA — ready to mobilize within 72 hours.
