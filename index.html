Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from clients — answered upfront.

How long does a website take?

Usually 3–7 days depending on the project scope and how quickly you provide your content and branding materials.

Can you customize the template?

Yes, every design can be fully customized — colors, fonts, layout, content, and images are all tailored to your brand.

Do I need to provide content?

Yes. You can provide your logo, images, and company information. I'll help structure everything into a professional layout.

Can I get a custom domain later?

Yes. Your site can start on GitHub Pages and be connected to a custom domain whenever you're ready.
